The Mall

  • Originality: The game is based on my first iteration.
  • Technical Merit: I added the ability to add player names. Player names and health are displayed on the screen and update as players take damage.
      Player names now appear in the text rather than "player 1"
      The fight scene against the king considers player health, player weapons, and randomness to decide if they win or lose
      Before, the king fight scene was dependend on the first or section option

      CobraPicture
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This game style is based on the Organ Trail. The other game that is similar to this is the previous iteration.
  • High Concept: A group of travelers travels throught an mall in Sedona, Arizona trying to find food and survive the Mall.
  • Theme: Challenge (theme) used: Multiple Players required to play.
  • Prototype Goal: This iteration I got player names and player health to work, as well as displaying the players names and health on each scene.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should begin to understand the story and think through each of their decisons.
  • Gameplay: Two to four players are required. Players assign themselves a number between one and four. Players begin with a health of three. If a player takes damage, they take one damage point.
      The players have to agree on an option before proceeding, on one player should make the decision.
  • Strategies: Players should take their time and consider their options.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The game takes place in Sedona, Arizona with a group of travelers in search for food. They discover a mall they believed to be abandonded, but rather encourter a tribe that they must face off with.
  • Target Audience: Players who enjoy a simplistic game with an enjoyable and original storyline.
  • Play Time: Play time is between 5 and 20 minutes.
  • Version: 2.0.0
  • Known Bugs Options with long text have to be clicked on the bottom row of the text. Clicking on the top rows of the text do nothing.
