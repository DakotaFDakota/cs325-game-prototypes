  • High Concept: A game of returning carts to the cart return.
  • Theme: Challenge (theme) used: Can be played in exactly 20 seconds.
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Mouse, Randomness: a lot of randomness, Play Style: Collector, Genre: Puzzle
  • Prototype Goal: This game learns how to work with timed events.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should get a rewarding experience by returning all of the carts to the return, showing the world they are a good person.
  • Gameplay: Players have 20 seconds to collect all of the carts, by clicking on them, which returns it to the cart return. By collecting of the carts, they win, but they must do so before the timer expires.
  • Strategies: Players should work to quickly click each cart.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The player is a customer at a shopping center and discovers many carts throughout the parking lot. The player tries to return all of the carts to the return.
  • Target Audience: Target Audience is players who want a short game that is fun and engaging.
  • Play Time: 20 seconds.
