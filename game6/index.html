The Mall

  • Originality: The entire story line was created by me. I designed each possible and step you can take and drew the button the first screen.
  • Technical Merit: The game uses a number of scenes to switch between "levels". Each possible option leads the player to a new scene which has its own text and options.
      The scenes can pass information between them. For example, if you pick up the crowbar, you keep the crowbar for the duration of the game
      and having the crowbar on a later level is beneficial.
      There are elements of randomness, which player will take damage from an incident that causes damage.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I want to have the game take in players names and keep track of players health, rather than outputting "player 1", or "player with the least amount of health."
      I want to have the game display player's name and health on the scene.
      I plan to add some pictures to the game every once in a while to spice up the scenes so they are not all plain text.
      I plan to change some the color of the text between the game text and the options so they do not blend together.
  • Prototype Assets: I created the button on the first scene.
      The win game music, Sedona by HoundmouthYoutube
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This game style is based on the Organ Trail. The game also has the same style as Dungeons and Dragons, with the game acting as the Dungeon Master.
  • High Concept: A group of travelers travels throught an mall in Sedona, Arizona trying to find food and survive the Mall.
  • Theme: Challenge (theme) used: Multiple Players required to play.
  • Prototype Goal: This game gave me an oppurtunity to work with multiple scenes, as well as passing data between scenes.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should begin to understand the story and think through each of their decisons.
  • Gameplay: Two to four players are required. Players assign themselves a number between one and four. Players begin with a health of three. If a player takes damage, they take one damage point.
      The players have to agree on an option before proceeding, on one player should make the decision.
  • Strategies: Players should take their time and consider their options.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The game takes place in Sedona, Arizona with a group of travelers in search for food. They discover a mall they believed to be abandonded, but rather encourter a tribe that they must face off with.
  • Target Audience: Players who enjoy a simplistic game with an enjoyable and original storyline.
  • Play Time: Play time is between 5 and 20 minutes.
  • Version: 1.0.0
  • Known Bugs Options with long text have to be clicked on the bottom row of the text. Clickign on the top rows of the text do nothing.
